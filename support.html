About the App

Vibe is a simple mood tracker that allows you to log your emotions with a single tap and view your emotional patterns over time.

In-App Purchases

Vibe offers a one-time purchase to unlock premium features, including unlimited mood logs, full insights, and all emotion videos.

Restore Purchases

If you have already purchased, you can restore your purchase by tapping the “Restore Purchase” option inside the app.

Data & Privacy

All data is stored locally on your device. No account is required.
